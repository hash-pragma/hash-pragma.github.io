Child pornography is criminal offence and a repercussion of child trafficking. Tens of thousands of videos are uploaded on many websites throughout the world in a day. This is a global social problem which needs to be addressed using latest technologies.

We are focusing on solving one the social problem existing in the digital world i.e. Child Pornography. It is criminal offence and various laws are made for it.

#pragma bans on uploading of such video and spreading them over the internet. Filtering videos on Porn websites manually is very slow process which leads to gateway for people to easily upload videos which are against such law.

By analysing the content of video before it get published, our product checks on such restricted content and discard the upload if found. It also tracks the criminal by using his location, and various analyses are carried out on this data to help crime investigator for tracking such people.

Services

Video Analysis

We intend to prevent further addition of such videos on the internet and even remove such contents or report to the authority. We have developed an video analysis platform, which internally works on advanced image recognition and age detection using machine learning and neural-network concepts. It recognizes if a given pornographic video has child actors involved via age analysis of all actors. The recognized videos have certain accuracy associated.

Banning the video

The video is then further reported to the website/manual testers for recheck. And if the child pornographic contents are found in the video it is then banned.

Tracking the uploaders

We further analyze who is uploading the video, from which area it is being uploaded and then mark certain areas as hotspots for such activities. This is helpful in tracking the uploaders. This location tracking further helps us to predict the probablity of a video being a child pornographic video if it was being uploaded by a particular user from a particular location.
